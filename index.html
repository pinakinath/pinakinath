About

Before my current position, I was an NBHM Postdoctoral Fellow in Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru.

My research lies in algebraic geometry with emphasis on algebraic groups, flag varieties, Schubert varieties, Bott–Samelson varieties, toric and spherical varieties, combinatorics of Weyl groups, and geometric aspects of representation theory.

Research Interests

  • Algebraic groups and flag varieties.
  • Schubert varieties and their resolutions (Bott–Samelson).
  • Toric methods, torus quotients and toric Richardson varieties.
  • Spherical varieties and combinatorics of Weyl groups.
  • Geometric representation theory and homogeneous vector bundles.
